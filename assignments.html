Tools Make the Man

Your goal this week is to get comfortable with the tools that we will use in this course. Take time to use firebug to look at requests and responses for various web sites. Get familiar with the information that is provided within firebug. Also, take some time to explore Eclipse and use it to make some simple web pages. Also, I highly recommend creating your own github page if you don't already have one and practicing doing pushes and pulls from your repository.

Collaboration

Your job this week is to find time to work with your team to begin putting together your part of the project. You will be given time in class to organize a plan; however, you will need to find time to create your portion outside of class. The goal of this homework is to get you to be able to work effectively as a team to complete a task.

Finish the Project

This week you will have received feedback on the progress of your project. If your part requires additional work this is the time to do it. Collaborate with your team to ensure that you have completed your part of the project by the last class. Your part MUST be complete before the last class so that we can complete and deploy the web application.

But Class Is Over...

CONGRATULATIONS!!!! You have completed the course. Please fill out the class survey that has been sent to you. At this point you should be thinking about your next steps. If you are interested in front-end UI development, you should consider taking the Javascript 101 course. If you are interested in backend development there are many backend language courses to choose from. Take a look at the available courses on the Code Camp website to find what interests you.
